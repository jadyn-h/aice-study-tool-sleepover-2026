About AICE Study Tool

This site is meant to help high schools students study for their AICE Exams by setting up small study sessions

The planned sujects are AICE US history and Marine Science A Level

Planned features include: Timer for study sessions, mini mock sessions where one question is viewed, with randomized questions

For history, I want to separate content by chapters based on current sylabus
For Marine I want to focus on chapter and experiment questions
There will be an answer reval option
